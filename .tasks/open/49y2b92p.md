Requires: (1) new pkg/policyserver/plugin/ package with proto + GRPCTokenValidator + lifecycle management, (2) add evaluator.NewWithValidator() to decouple OIDC from constructor, (3) token_validator_plugin config field in ServerConfig, (4) wire through cmd/epithet/policy.go.
